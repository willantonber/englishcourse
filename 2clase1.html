💬 Conversación Integrada: Planificando con el Clima

Maria: Hey Carlos, what's the weather forecast for this weekend?

Carlos: I think it will be sunny on Saturday, but rainy on Sunday. The temperature will be around 25 degrees.

Maria: Perfect! My family wants to visit the park on Saturday. We can have a picnic with some food.

Carlos: That sounds nice! What will you do if it rains on Sunday?

Maria: Well, we can stay at home. Maybe watch a movie or play games in the living room.

Carlos: Do you need to take any special clothes for the weather?

Maria: Yes, I'll wear a hat for the sun, and bring an umbrella just in case.

Practica esta conversación:
