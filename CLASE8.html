Ejemplos

Frases útiles para pedir direcciones:

  • How do I get to...? (¿Cómo llego a...?)
  • Where is...? (¿Dónde está...?)
  • Is it far from here? (¿Está lejos de aquí?)
  • Can you show me on the map? (¿Puede mostrarme en el mapa?)

Frases útiles para dar direcciones:

  • Go straight ahead (Siga derecho)
  • Turn left/right (Gire a la izquierda/derecha)
  • It's on your left/right (Está a su izquierda/derecha)
  • It's next to... (Está al lado de...)
  • It's between... and... (Está entre... y...)

Preposiciones de movimiento y ubicación:

  • Go along the street (Siga por la calle)
  • Go across the bridge (Cruce el puente)
  • Go through the park (Atraviese el parque)
  • Go past the bank (Pase el banco)
  • Go towards the hospital (Diríjase hacia el hospital)
  • Go up/down the street (Suba/baje por la calle)

Ejemplos de diálogos:

Tourist: Excuse me, how do I get to the museum?

Local: Go straight ahead for two blocks, then turn left. It's on your right, next to the library.

Person A: Where is the nearest bank?

Person B: Cross the street, go past the supermarket, and it's on the corner of Main Street and First Avenue.

Driver: How can I reach the highway?

Passerby: Continue straight, then take the second right. Follow the signs to the highway entrance.

Diálogo: Perdido en la Ciudad

Escucha y lee el siguiente diálogo:

Tourist: Excuse me, I think I'm lost. How can I reach the city center?

Local: You need to stop at the traffic light and turn right. Then follow this street for about 10 minutes.

Tourist: Should I wait for a bus or is it walking distance?

Local: You can ride the bus number 5, or just walk. Don't cross the bridge, that will take you to the wrong side.

Tourist: Thank you! I don't want to miss my meeting. What time does the bus return to this area?

Local: The last bus returns at midnight. But you can always take a taxi if you close your meeting late.
