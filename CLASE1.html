Contenido Principal

Verbo "To Be"

El verbo "to be" significa "ser" o "estar". En presente simple:

  • I am (Yo soy/estoy)
  • You are (Tú eres/estás)
  • He/She/It is (Él/Ella/Ello es/está)
  • We are (Nosotros somos/estamos)
  • You are (Vosotros sois/estáis)
  • They are (Ellos son/están)

Saludos

  • Hello / Hi (Hola)
  • Good morning (Buenos días)
  • Good afternoon (Buenas tardes)
  • Good evening (Buenas noches)

Presentaciones

  • My name is [Nombre] (Mi nombre es)
  • I am from [Lugar] (Soy de)
  • Nice to meet you (Encantado de conocerte)

Despedidas

  • Goodbye / Bye (Adiós)
  • See you later (Nos vemos luego)
  • Take care (Cuídate)

10 Ejemplos

  1. Hi, how are you? - I'm fine, thanks.
  2. Good morning! My name is Anna.
  3. Hello, I'm from Spain. Nice to meet you.
  4. Goodbye, see you later!
  5. Take care, have a nice day.
  6. Good evening, how's it going? - Pretty good.
  7. Bye-bye, it was nice seeing you.
  8. Hi there! What's your name? - I'm John.
  9. Good afternoon, pleased to meet you.
  10. Farewell, talk to you soon.
