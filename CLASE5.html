Expresiones útiles en restaurantes

Para ordenar
Problemas
Para pagar

Frases útiles para ordenar comida:

  • I would like... (Me gustaría...)
  • Can I have...? (¿Puedo tener...?)
  • What do you recommend? (¿Qué recomienda?)
  • I'll have... (Voy a tomar...)
  • Could I see the menu? (¿Podría ver el menú?)

Frases para resolver problemas:

  • This is not what I ordered. (Esto no es lo que pedí)
  • My food is cold. (Mi comida está fría)
  • Could I change this? (¿Podría cambiar esto?)
  • We've been waiting a long time. (Hemos esperado mucho tiempo)

Frases para pagar:

  • Can I have the bill? (¿Me puede traer la cuenta?)
  • We'd like to pay. (Nos gustaría pagar)
  • Do you accept credit cards? (¿Aceptan tarjetas de crédito?)
  • Is service included? (¿El servicio está incluido?)

Diálogo: En el Restaurante

Escucha y lee el siguiente diálogo:

Waiter: Good evening. Welcome to our restaurant.

Customer: Good evening. We'd like to buy dinner here. Can you serve us at that table?

Waiter: Of course. Here are the menus. I'll send someone to take your order.

Customer: Thank you. We expect to try your special pasta.

Waiter: Excellent choice. How long will you stay in town?

Customer: We're here for three days. We don't want to die without trying the local food!

Waiter: (Laughs) I'm sure you'll win with our dishes. Enjoy your meal!
